The MAIA viewer facilitates easy access to information in SEEA accounts. It makes our data available for analysis, reporting and decision-making.

The tool helps you to combine data and obtain data per user-defined polygons or per administrative unit like communities, provinces etc.

Registered users can access the tool here http://app.maiaportal.eu/.
